## Technical Developments

New AI models, products, features, **popular** research that make the news and become trending--

from (but not limited to) frontier AI labs and AI tech companies, orgs, figures, such as:

**Frontier AI Labs:** OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Anthropic, xAI, Nvidia, Meta, DeepSeek, Z.AI, Moonshot AI, Alibaba, etc.
**Frontier AI tech Companies:** Nvidia, Google, Microsoft, Apple, Amazon, Meta, Tesla, Palantir, etc.
**AI Figures:** Industry leaders like Sam Altman, Dario Amodei, Demis Hassabis, Elon Musk, Mark Zuckerberg, Sundar Pichai, Satya Nadella; legendary developers and researchers, etc.

**Important:** These are just some examples, any trending and popular news on technical AI development (including AI powered robotics, physical AI, etc.) are in scope

## Non Technical Developments

Trending and new AI related news, headline, quotes, etc., involving AI in **ALL industries** and aspects, especially in tech industry, business, startups, VCs

**Important:** These are just some examples, any trending and popular news on non-technical AI development (including AI powered robotics, physical AI, etc.) are in scope

## Geographic Scope

The focus should primarily involve these 3 geographic locations:
United States (highest priority), China (mid priority), Europe (lower priority)
